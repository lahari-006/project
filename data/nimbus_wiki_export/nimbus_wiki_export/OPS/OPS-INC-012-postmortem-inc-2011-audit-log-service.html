Postmortem: INC-2011 - audit-log-service

Summary

Severity: SEV3. Duration: 25 minutes. Service affected: audit-log-service.

Root Cause

The incident was caused when a expired TLS certificate was not rotated in time.

Impact

Approximately 1% of requests to audit-log-service returned errors or elevated latency during the incident window.

Resolution

The on-call engineer mitigated by rolling back the most recent deploy and restarting affected pods. Full resolution confirmed via dashboard recovery.

Follow-up Actions

  • Add a regression test covering this failure mode
  • Add an alert threshold to catch this earlier next time
