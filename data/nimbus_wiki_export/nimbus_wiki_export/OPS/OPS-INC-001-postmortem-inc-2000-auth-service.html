Postmortem: INC-2000 - auth-service

Summary

Severity: SEV1. Duration: 55 minutes. Service affected: auth-service.

Root Cause

The incident was caused when a bad deploy introduced a null-pointer bug in the request handler.

Impact

Approximately 5% of requests to auth-service returned errors or elevated latency during the incident window.

Resolution

The on-call engineer mitigated by rolling back the most recent deploy and restarting affected pods. Full resolution confirmed via dashboard recovery.

Follow-up Actions

  • Add a regression test covering this failure mode
  • Add an alert threshold to catch this earlier next time
