VPN Setup Guide


1. Install the Nimbus VPN client from software.nimbuscloud.io/vpn
2. Log in with your SSO credentials
3. Select the "us-corp" profile
4. Connect - you should see a green status indicator

If connection fails, confirm MFA is enrolled (see ITS-004) and contact #it-help on Slack.
