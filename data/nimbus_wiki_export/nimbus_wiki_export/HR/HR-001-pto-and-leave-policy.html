PTO and Leave Policy

Effective February 2026. This is the current, authoritative version of the leave policy for all Nimbus Cloud Systems employees.

Overview

Nimbus Cloud Systems provides a flexible paid time off (PTO) program for all full-time employees. This policy replaces all prior PTO policies, including any versions published before January 2026.

Accrual

TenureAnnual PTO DaysAccrual Rate
0-2 years20 days1.67 days/month
2-5 years24 days2.0 days/month
5+ years28 days2.33 days/month

Carryover

Employees may carry over up to 10 unused PTO days into the next calendar year. Any days beyond that cap are forfeited on December 31st unless local law requires payout.

Sick Leave

Sick leave is tracked separately from PTO. All employees receive 10 paid sick days per year, which do not roll over.

Requesting Time Off

Submit PTO requests through the HR portal at least 5 business days in advance for planned leave. Manager approval is required for absences longer than 5 consecutive days.
