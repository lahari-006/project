Expense Reimbursement Policy

All business expenses must be submitted through the Expensify portal within 30 days of the purchase date. Receipts are required for any expense over $25.

Reimbursable Categories

CategoryMonthly CapApproval Needed
Client meals$300Manager
Conference travel$2,500/eventDirector
Software/tools$100None (auto-approved)
Team offsites$150/personManager

Non-Reimbursable Items

Alcohol at solo meals, personal entertainment, and first-class flight upgrades are not reimbursable under any circumstance.
