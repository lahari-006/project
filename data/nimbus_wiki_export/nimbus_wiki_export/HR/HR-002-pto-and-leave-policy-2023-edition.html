PTO and Leave Policy (2023 Edition)

Effective April 2023.

Overview

Nimbus Cloud Systems provides a flexible paid time off (PTO) program for all full-time employees.

Accrual

TenureAnnual PTO DaysAccrual Rate
0-2 years15 days1.25 days/month
2-5 years18 days1.5 days/month
5+ years22 days1.83 days/month

Carryover

Employees may carry over up to 5 unused PTO days into the next calendar year. Unused days beyond that are forfeited on December 31st.

Sick Leave

Sick leave is bundled into the general PTO allotment above; there is no separate sick leave bucket.

Requesting Time Off

Submit PTO requests through your manager via email at least 10 business days in advance.
