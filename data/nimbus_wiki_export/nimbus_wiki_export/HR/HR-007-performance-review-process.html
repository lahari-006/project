Performance Review Process

Nimbus runs performance reviews twice a year: a lightweight mid-year check-in in June, and a full calibrated review cycle in December that determines compensation changes.

Review Components

  1. Self-assessment (employee-authored)
  2. Peer feedback (2-4 peers, manager-selected)
  3. Manager assessment and calibration

Rating Scale

RatingMeaning
ExceptionalTop 5%, significantly exceeds expectations
StrongConsistently exceeds expectations
MeetsSolid, reliable performance
DevelopingBelow expectations, improvement plan initiated
