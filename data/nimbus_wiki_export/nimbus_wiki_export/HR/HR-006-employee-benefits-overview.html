Employee Benefits Overview

This page summarizes core benefits available to full-time Nimbus Cloud Systems employees. For plan-specific details, see the linked provider documents.

Health Coverage

Nimbus covers 90% of medical premiums and 100% of dental and vision premiums for employees, and 60% for dependents.

Retirement

401(k) with a 4% company match, fully vested from day one.

Other Perks

  • $1,000 annual learning & development budget
  • Free gym membership reimbursement up to $60/month
  • Annual company offsite
