Remote Work Policy

Nimbus Cloud Systems operates as a hybrid-first company. Employees on hybrid teams are expected in the office 2 days per week (Tuesday and Thursday are core in-office days company-wide). Fully remote roles are designated at the time of hire and noted in the offer letter.

Home Office Stipend

Remote and hybrid employees receive a one-time $750 home office setup stipend and a $50/month connectivity allowance.

Working Across Time Zones

Teams spanning more than 3 time zones should establish a documented core overlap window of at least 4 hours per day.
