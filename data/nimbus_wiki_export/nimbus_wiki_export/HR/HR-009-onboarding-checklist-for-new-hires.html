Onboarding Checklist for New Hires

Before Day One

  • IT provisions laptop and accounts (see ITS-002)
  • Manager schedules first-week 1:1s
  • Buddy assigned from the team

Week One

  • Complete compliance training modules
  • Set up VPN access (see ITS-001)
  • Review team runbooks relevant to your role

30/60/90

Managers should hold formal check-ins at 30, 60, and 90 days to review ramp progress against the onboarding plan.
