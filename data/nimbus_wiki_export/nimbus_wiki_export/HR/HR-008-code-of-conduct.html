Code of Conduct

All employees, contractors, and interns are expected to act with integrity, respect, and professionalism in all interactions, whether in person, remote, or on internal tools.

Prohibited Conduct

Harassment, discrimination, retaliation, and conflicts of interest that are not disclosed to People Ops are strictly prohibited and may result in termination.

Reporting

Concerns can be reported confidentially via the Ethics Hotline or directly to People Ops. Retaliation against good-faith reporters is a fireable offense.
