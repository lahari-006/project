Parental Leave Policy

Nimbus Cloud Systems offers 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave to all employees, regardless of gender, following the birth, adoption, or fostering of a child. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event and can be split into two blocks of at least 2 weeks each.

Eligibility

All full-time employees are eligible starting on their first day of employment. Part-time employees (under 30 hrs/week) receive prorated leave.

Job Protection

Employees are guaranteed the right to return to the same or an equivalent role and level upon completion of leave.
