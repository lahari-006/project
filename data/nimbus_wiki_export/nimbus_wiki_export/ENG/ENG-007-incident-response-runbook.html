Incident Response Runbook

Severity Levels

SevDefinitionResponse Time
SEV1Full outage or data lossImmediate, page on-call
SEV2Major feature degraded15 min
SEV3Minor issue, workaround existsNext business day

Process

  1. Declare incident in #incidents Slack channel with severity
  2. Assign an Incident Commander (IC)
  3. IC coordinates mitigation; do not attempt to write the postmortem during active mitigation
  4. Once resolved, file a postmortem within 48 hours
