Deployment Process (Legacy Jenkins Flow)

This process is deprecated and no longer in use, retained for historical reference only.

Pipeline

Deploys were triggered through Jenkins. Engineers manually kicked off a Jenkins job after merging to master, and production promotion required filing a change-request ticket in Jira, approved by a release manager.


# Old Jenkins CLI trigger (no longer functional)
jenkins-cli build deploy-prod-job -p SERVICE=billing-service -p VERSION=2.1.0

Rollback

Rollbacks required re-running the previous Jenkins build manually, typically taking 15-20 minutes.
