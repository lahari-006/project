Secrets Management Policy

All secrets (API keys, DB credentials, tokens) must be stored in HashiCorp Vault. Hardcoding secrets in code or config files is strictly prohibited and will fail CI secret-scanning.

Secrets are rotated automatically every 90 days for production credentials.
