On-call Rotation Policy

Each service team maintains a weekly on-call rotation via PagerDuty. On-call engineers receive a $200/week stipend and may take a floating day off the following week.

Escalation

If the primary on-call does not acknowledge within 10 minutes, PagerDuty automatically escalates to the secondary, then to the engineering manager.
